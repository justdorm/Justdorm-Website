Hi, I'm Justin Dormitzer - a New York City based designer, animator, and interactive artist on a mission to transport you to other worlds through my work. I hold an MFA degree in Computer Arts at the School of Visual Arts with a focus on motion graphics and experimental art. My approach to art and design is to combine technical expertise with a vivid imagination, resulting in pieces that are both visually striking and sensorily immersive. I enjoy blending traditional and digital mediums to create works that are rich in both texture and color.
