Hi, I'm Justin - a Brooklyn Based Designer and Animator on a mission to transport you to other worlds through my work. I'm currently pursuing an MFA in Computer Arts at the School of Visual Arts with a focus on motion graphics. My approach to art and design is to combine technical expertise with a vivid imagination, resulting in pieces that are both visually stunning and emotionally impactful. I enjoy blending digital and traditional mediums and invite you to browse my portfolio and experience the magic of my creations for yourself.
