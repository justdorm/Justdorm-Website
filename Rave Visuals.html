Rave Visuals

This is a complilation of my work as a VJ from raves at various venues in Queens hosted by Haustoria. I create systems that are both audio-reactive and MIDI controlled in TouchDesigner, connecting my PC to the DJ booth to directly drive my visuals based on the sound waves from the music. I bind different parameters of my visuals to sliders and buttons on my MIDI controller to allow for variation and art direction in my performance.
