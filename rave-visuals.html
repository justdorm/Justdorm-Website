Case study 07 — Creative Technology

Rave Visuals

Live-rendered, audio-reactive visuals driven straight from the DJ booth

Role
VJ & Creative technologist
Tools
TouchDesigner · MIDI · Depth Camera
Venue
Haustoria — venues across Queens, NYC

Sound you can see

This is a compilation of my work as a VJ at raves across various venues in Queens, hosted by Haustoria. I build systems in TouchDesigner that are both audio-reactive and MIDI-controlled, connecting my PC directly to the DJ booth so the sound waves from the music drive the visuals in real time.

How it works

Different parameters of each visual system are both audio-reactive and bound to sliders and buttons on a MIDI controller, allowing for variation and live art direction over the course of a set — I perform the visuals live; nothing is pre-rendered.
